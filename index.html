Decide when AI should answer, refuse, or escalate — before mistakes create real consequences.

Why AI Breaks in Real Operations

Most AI systems are built to answer. In real-world workflows, that is where problems begin.

  • Systems respond even when they should not
  • Confident but incorrect answers slip through
  • No clear boundary between safe automation and risk

In high-risk environments, even small mistakes can create financial, operational, or compliance issues.

How Decisions Are Controlled

Structured Request Relevant Knowledge Confidence Check Risk Evaluation Final Decision

Decision outcomes:

  • Allowed — Safe to proceed
  • Refused — Stopped due to risk
  • Escalated — Sent for human review

What the System Controls

  • When AI is allowed to respond
  • When responses must be refused
  • When decisions should be escalated
  • Policy-aware decision boundaries
  • Audit traceability of every decision

Where It Works

  • Customer support workflows
  • Finance and refund decisions
  • Internal operational decisions
  • Compliance and policy validation
